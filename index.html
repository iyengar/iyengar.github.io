Hi! I'm Sameer.

My mission is to spread happiness with technology.

I'm the co-founder of Beautylish. We're building a compelling new retail model centered around providing a high level of personal service. We help you figure out which products will work well for you and teach you how to get the most out of them.

I've previously worked on monetization at YouTube, data analytics at Google, missile interceptors at Lockheed Martin and medical sensor device research at UC Berkeley.

Teaching

I'm a strong believer in education and I enjoy teaching:
  • I taught Intro to Computer Science at Balboa High school in Fall 2014 as part of TEALS.
  • I mentor low-income, high-achieving high school students with Minds Matter of San Francisco.
  • I was a teaching assistant for CS 61C the third introductory CS class at UC Berkeley in Fall 2006. You can watch a younger, more handsome version of me give a lecture about networking.
  • I helped develop the Weiner Lecture Archives, an archive of UC Berkeley CS lectures and corresponding lecture notes.

Other Fun Projects

  • Online since 1999 - My first project was a site that linked to popular Java games. Thanks to the internet archive, you can still see what an early version and a later revision looked like.
  • Notizi: Social RSS reader that recommended news articles based on things a user's friends liked. I co-founded Notizi along with Casey Ho. Watch a screencast of one of our early versions.
  • Smartizen: Application that determined a representatives stance on major issues by analyzing their voting record in congress. We tagged bills into major topic areas and displayed how each representative voted on bills in that topic.
  • Popular Now: A Google Search Trends and Yahoo! Boss Mashup that tweeted out news stories based on what people were searching for.
